The Sparse Enrichment Analysis for CUT&RUN (SEACR) software package was 
developed by Michael Meers of the Henikoff Lab and is described  in:
Meers, M.P., Tenenbaum, D. & Henikoff, S. Peak calling by Sparse Enrichment 
Analysis for CUT&RUN chromatin profiling. Epigenetics & Chromatin 12, 42 (2019). 
https://doi.org/10.1186/s13072-019-0287-4

This software was acquired from: https://github.com/FredHutch/SEACR
Last Updated on 2020-08-11

Many thanks to Dr. Meers and the Henikoff Group for their permission 
to redstribute this software with CUT&RUN-Flow, and their strong
efforts to facilitate open, reproducible science.
